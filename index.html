1. Fundamental Analysis Projects

  • Piotroski F-score - Calculates the Piotroski F-score of a given stock to assess its financial strength.
  • Altman Z-Score - Calculates the Altman Z-Score of a given manufacturing firm to assess its likelihood of bankruptcy.
  • Beneish M-score - Calculates the Beneish M-score to assess the likelihood of financial statement manipulation by a firm.

2. Fama-French models

  • Fama-French 3 factor model - The original ff-3 factor model for US stocks.
  • Fama-French 5 factor model - The ff-5 factor model for US stocks.
  • Fama-French 4 factor model - The ff-4 factor model for Indian stocks.
  • Is illiquidity factored in a stock return? - Amihud illiquidity measure and ff-factors regressed against excess returns of a stock.

3. Market Characteristics

  • Post-Earnings Announcement Drift - PEAD phenomenon is looked at.
  • Market Health vs IPO listing - Compares the markets trend with the IPOs listed.
  • Socio-economic effects on stock prices - Analyses the recent SBI electoral bond news and unfair advantages to companies.

4. Timeseries predictions

  • SARIMA model - Prediction of economic indicators using SARIMA model.
  • SARIMAX model - Prediction of economic indicators using SARIMA model with exogenous factors (SARIMAX).
  • Exponential Smoothing model - Prediction of economic indicators using ETS model.
  • ETS model with exogenous factors - Prediction of economic indicators using ETS model with exogenous factors.

5. Credit Scoring

  • Credit Classification - Runs a Regression against financial metrics and classifies a company under a rating.

6. Portfolio Management

  • Markowitz model - Get a portfolio with minimum risk or with maximum sharpe ratio using this model.
  • Risk parity portfolio - Every stock contributes equally to the overall portfolio risk.
  • Hierarchy Risk Parity - Every classified hierarchy group contributes equally to the overall portfolio risk.

7. Derivatives Pricing

  • Black Scholes - Pricing options with classic Black Scholes model.
  • Black Scholes across expiries - Pricing options with classic Black Scholes model across different expiries.
  • Value prediction by regressing greeks - Value predicted by using calculated greeks and spot price.
  • Binomial Pricing - Value predicted by using Binomial Pricing model.
